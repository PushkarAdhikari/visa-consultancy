Why Study in Australia?

According to some surveys, there were almost 1,92,460 international students who enrolled in Australian universities on 1st September 2004. Some reports also reveal that the number of full degree students increased by 51.9% in India alone.

Australian qualifications are recognized worldwide for their excellent and high quality education system.

Graduates from Australia are successful and easily accepted at international universities for postgraduate studies.

International students can work part-time up to 20 hours per week to earn money and gain work experience.

Scholarship opportunities are available for international students.

Australia is a multi-cultural, safe, sophisticated, friendly and harmonious society to live and learn.

Universities meet strict accreditation requirements and educational standards by government authority.

Tuition fees and accommodation costs are comparatively less than USA and UK. Great value for money.

Australia is extremely diverse and welcoming. The international experience boosts your resume and future job opportunities.

Australia at a Glance

  • Capital: Canberra
  • Currency: Australian Dollar (AUD)
  • Language: English
  • Population: ~26 Million
  • Top Universities: ANU, Melbourne, Sydney, UNSW, Monash
  • Part-time Work: Up to 20 hrs/week
  • PR Pathway: Yes (Subclass 189, 190, 491)
  • Intakes: Feb & July (major)

Living in Australia

Australia offers a vibrant lifestyle with world-class amenities. Here's what you can expect:

Sports & Recreation

Australians love outdoor activities and sports. The country has over 120 national sports organizations along with thousands of club, regional and state bodies.

Transport

Modern transport systems including ferries, tramways, buses, trains, and two major national airlines. Metered taxicabs are available in all major towns.

Travel

Explore stunning natural beauty including Tasmanian Wilderness, Uluru, Kakadu, and the Great Barrier Reef during summer breaks.

Food

Australia is home to a variety of cuisines with the best quality vegetables, fruits, fish and meat. World-class restaurants, bistros, cafes, and global food franchises.
