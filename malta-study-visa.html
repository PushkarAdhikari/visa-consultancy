Malta Study Visa

British-based education system in the heart of the Mediterranean. Affordable, welcoming, and unforgettable.

The education system of Malta is considered one of the best for students seeking higher education in Europe or abroad. Sombir's Visa Consultancy is a higher education overseas consultant in Karnal and we have plenty of experience in Malta study Visa.

The travel destination of the country is amazing but that's not all as the country offers one of the best educational systems. Malta is also one of the few places where the British system is used to standardize the level of education of their institutes.

Study in Malta

A destination where tourists around the world are attracted to see its spectacular beauty, Malta is among the world's smallest countries with a dense population. Malta Island, Gozo and Comino are the three large islands incorporating a total of around 420,000 residents. Valletta is Malta's tiny capital and most of the colleges are situated around this capital.

Around 600 international students from around 80 countries are attracted to this destination every year. You can get well-regarded degrees by opting either full time or part-time programs in fields like Economics, Science and Technology, Arts, Management, Engineering, Health Sciences and many more.

Why Study in Malta?

90% of the population speaks English. No language barrier for international students.

People are friendly and welcoming. You don't need to learn the local language to communicate.

Wide range of programs — from Forestry, Agriculture to Fisheries, and much more.

Fee structure is very cheap compared to other European countries.

Courses for higher education are available and conducted in English.

Study visa process is simple. Working, studying and living in Malta is straightforward.

Food, culture, and weather are out of the world. A perfect blend of fun and study.

Rich history dating back to the Stone Age. A unique cultural experience.

Required Documents for Malta Study Visa

Sombir's Visa Consultancy frequently receives inquiries about documents required to enroll in universities and institutes of Malta. Below is the list of required documents:

  • IELTS scorecard (Students without IELTS are also eligible)
  • Recommendation letter (Mandatory for Malta study visa)
  • Photocopy of valid passport
  • Attested photocopies of 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • Attested Bachelor's degree marksheets (for Master's applicants)
  • Attested Post-graduate degree certificates (for Doctorate applicants)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Candidate's CV / Resume

About Malta

The surface area of Malta is around 316 sq. km and the country is filled with many historical events. A couple of rulers and nations fought wars and tried to control it before being dethroned by those who ruled it for a long time. The British arrived during the Stone Age and handled the island from 1814 to 1964.

Despite its rich history going back to ancient times, Malta has faced many events and incidents yet still holds itself together while moving with the world.
