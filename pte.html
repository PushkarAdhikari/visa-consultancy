PTE Coaching

Master the Pearson Test of English with our computer-based training and expert guidance.

PTE which is also known as Pearson Test of English (PTE) is a computer-based English language test accepted by thousands of universities and governments worldwide. The test measures your English ability in four key areas: Speaking, Listening, Reading, and Writing.

Unlike other English proficiency tests, PTE is entirely computer-based and uses AI scoring, making it one of the most objective and unbiased English language assessments available. The test score ranges from 10 to 90 points, and our students consistently achieve scores between 65 and 79.
