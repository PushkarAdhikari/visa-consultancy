Cyprus Study Visa

Affordable European education with a mild Mediterranean climate and over 300 days of sunshine.

Cyprus is a diverse country that offers a number of opportunities for good living standards, career goals and some other personal benefits. It is one of the best countries in Europe where people can live happily due to friendly culture along with easy migration.

Being the leading edge in technology, the country's different economic sectors like banking, telecommunication, tourism, etc. enjoy a high living standard. The country is full of natural beauty and has one of the healthiest and favorable environments in the whole world — free from many kinds of pollution.

Cyprus is one of the best destinations for students who want to study abroad and many international students come from across the world. Cyprus provides the best quality of education along with affordable tuition fees and excellent facilities. Even though Cyprus is associated with the Bologna system, the majority of colleges implement the American system of education.

Why Study in Cyprus?

There are numerous reasons why students should pick Cyprus for their further education abroad. Currently, there are over more than 40,000 international students studying in the universities and institutes of Cyprus.

Cyprus colleges have foreign students from over 25 countries including India, Pakistan, Russia, China, UAE, and more.

Colleges provide airport-pickup, housing assistance, city tours, orientation, and much more for international students.

High-quality education with a flexible curriculum for students.

Sponsorship is not required. Simple and easy admission procedures.

Very low fee structure. Degrees from Cyprus colleges are accepted worldwide.

Many colleges don't require passing any English Proficiency test.

  • Capital: Nicosia
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Languages: Greek, Turkish, English
  • Population: ~1.2 Million
  • Climate: Mediterranean (300+ sunny days/year)
  • Education System: American / Bologna
  • Visa: Simple application process
  • Intl. Students: 40,000+

About Cyprus

From a Mediterranean island to the mythical birthplace of Aphrodite, Cyprus combines natural beauty and a friendly population with rich culture and archaeological heritage. Greeks, Egyptians, Arabs, Venetians, British, and many others have left their legacy in the country today.

Cyprus is a stable and economically advanced region of the European Union and provides a stunning view of Western cities along with modern beach resorts built alongside traditional villages and monasteries on the mountains. The country is becoming an education and research center with constant investment from international bodies.

The climate is mild with over 300 days of sunlight every year, making it an ideal tourist destination all year round. More than 2.5 million tourists pick Cyprus as their holiday destination.
