Study in Canada

Canada possesses some of the world's top academic institutions and research facilities. Students get to learn in an effective academic environment where professors and other teaching faculty are supportive. In addition, students also get to live in classrooms full of diverse surroundings with students from all across the world.

Canada also has institutions which are recognized by the world. This helps graduates to join the workforce effortlessly. That's why the majority of students prefer to come to Canada rather than any other country.

Why Canada? Canadian universities degrees are considered equivalent to American degrees. The country has around 90 universities and 150 technical colleges and institutes offering numerous courses and programs.

Why Study in Canada?

Canada invests more in education than any other country. Top 3 in OECD rankings for education quality.

Colleges and universities are well-known for excellent education, safe campuses, and modern infrastructure.

Canada is a world leader in Information Technology revolution, ensuring students are ready for the 21st century.

Full range of undergraduates, graduates, and professional degree programs across 90+ universities.

World leader in Environmental industries, Mining tech, Biotechnology, Aerospace, and Software development.

Opportunities for co-operative education, international exchange programs, and extra-curricular activities.

Canadian students score among the top in Science, Mathematics, and Reading in PISA studies.

Canada offers the highest quality of life in the whole world according to U.N. rankings.

Living in Canada

It is a dream for Indian people to live in Canada and the reason is only because of its Lifestyle. People are happy in this country as there are multiple job opportunities, part-time works for students who want to cover their expenses, less population, and much more.

And that's not all; the country is also famous for their tourism as well. Places like Niagara Falls, Whistler for perfect Ski Resort, Quebec City to explore the influence of French culture, and much more. Hence, Canada has plenty of tourist places for explorer adventurous people.

Canada at a Glance

  • Capital: Ottawa
  • Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD)
  • Languages: English & French
  • Population: ~38 Million
  • Universities: 90+
  • Colleges: 150+
  • PR Pathway: Yes (Express Entry)
  • Study Permit: Up to 5 years

Climate

The climate varies drastically in Canada depending on the region. From lush vegetation of British Columbia west coast to the permanent frozen ice caps in the north. There are mainly four seasons, specifically in areas with more population including the US border.

Summer

35°C+

Daytime temperatures can reach 35 degrees and more

Winter

-15°C

Lowest temperatures can drop to -15 degrees in populated areas

The people of Canada have adapted well to the extreme climate of the country by installing heaters in houses and cars, and the same application is applied in the public transportation system as well. Spring and fall seasons offer moderate temperatures.
